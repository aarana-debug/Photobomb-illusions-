Frequently Asked

The 360° platform itself is approximately 4ft in diameter. We recommend a minimum of 10×10 feet of clear floor space to ensure guests can safely step on and off and that there's room for the arm to rotate. Ceiling height should be at least 8 feet.
We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance, especially for weekend dates which fill up quickly. However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings when availability allows — reach out and we'll do our best to make it happen.
Yes! All packages include a branded digital overlay. Gold and Platinum packages include a fully custom design featuring your name, event date, and any logo or graphic you'd like. We'll work with you in advance to create the perfect look.
Guests can receive their video instantly via QR code or text message right at the booth. They can then share directly to Instagram, TikTok, or any social platform. The full event gallery is also delivered to you within 48 hours.
Delivery within 40 miles is included in all packages. For events beyond that radius, a travel fee applies. We're happy to travel further for the right event — contact us with your location for a custom quote.
Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 14 days forfeit the deposit. We understand life happens — we're always happy to apply your deposit toward a rescheduled date.
